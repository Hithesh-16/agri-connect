Slot-Based Booking

For labor, professional services, vet visits, drone spraying. Half-day slots: Morning (6AM-12PM) and Afternoon (12PM-6PM). Full-day option available. Reflects rural work schedules, not urban 9-5.

Day-Based Booking

For machinery rental, transport. Multi-day continuous bookings (harvester for 3+ days). Minimum booking durations enforced (combine harvester = 1 day min, tractor = half-day min).

Recurring Bookings

Subscription-style for regular services: "Spray every 15 days", "Monthly soil test". Auto-renewal with skip/pause. Seasonal packages: Kharif (Jun-Oct) and Rabi (Oct-Mar) bundles.

Calendar Blocking Logic

Machinery Calendar

When booked: block booked days + transit days (time to move equipment). Add 1-day maintenance buffer between bookings. Overbooking prevention with real-time sync. Seasonal availability settings (harvester only Oct-Dec).

Labor Calendar

Team leader's calendar blocks entire team. Individual workers can set daily availability. QR check-in at farm (GPS verified). Substitute worker policy: team leader arranges replacement within 2 hours or fee is adjusted.

Payment Escrow Flow

Farmer pays → Platform holds in escrow → Service completed → Farmer confirms (or auto-confirm 24-48h) → Platform releases minus commission. For multi-day: milestone-based release (50% after day 1, 50% after completion).

Cancellation Policy

Free: 48h+ before (machinery), 24h+ (services). 50% refund: late cancellation. No refund: no-show. Weather: full refund or free reschedule. Provider cancel: auto-rebook + penalty to provider.

Payment Methods (Rural India Priority)

Primary. 70% users from non-Tier-1. PhonePe, Google Pay, BHIM. Intent + collect flows.

Cash + Digital Split

20-30% advance via UPI, balance in cash on completion. Essential for trust.

Section 07 — User Roles & Features

Role-Based Feature Plan

Three distinct user roles with tailored dashboards, features, and workflows. Every interaction designed for rural users with limited connectivity and varying literacy levels.

Search & Discovery

GPS-based "services near me" with configurable radius (5/10/25/50 km). Hierarchical category browsing. Filter by price, rating, distance, availability. Voice search in 10+ languages via Bhashini API. Icon-heavy UI to reduce language dependency.

Booking & Scheduling

Visual calendar with real-time provider availability. Select provider + preferred date(s). Instant booking for available slots. Request-based booking for high-demand periods. Booking history with "Book Again" quick action.

Job Posting (Reverse Marketplace)

Post a requirement: "Need 5 laborers for cotton picking, 10 acres, 3 days, starting April 15". Vendors/laborers see and bid with their rates. Farmer compares bids, selects best. Like Upwork but for agriculture.

Direct Hiring

Browse vendor listings, view profiles/ratings/photos. "Book Now" for instant booking. "Contact Provider" for custom needs (conversation tracked on-platform to prevent bypass). Save favorites for quick rebooking.

Market Intelligence

Live mandi prices with trend charts. Price alerts (above/below thresholds). Weather-integrated farm calendar. Crop advisory with disease detection. Government scheme eligibility checker.

Payments & Records

UPI-first payment flow. Payment history with SMS/WhatsApp receipts. Spending summary by category and season. GST invoices for input purchases. Insurance claim tracking.

Profile & Service Management

Multi-service listings per vendor (tractor owner + transport). Service photos/videos. Pricing: per hour/acre/day/unit. Service area radius. Equipment details (make, model, HP). Pricing: fixed, negotiable, or bid-based.

Availability Calendar

Toggle-based calendar (available/unavailable per day). Auto-blocking on booking. Seasonal availability (harvester only Oct-Dec). Bulk update (mark entire month). SMS-based updates: reply "OFF 15-APR" to block a date.

Order Management

Incoming booking requests with accept/reject. Active jobs dashboard. Completion flow: started → in progress → completed → proof photos. View and respond to farmer job postings/bids.

Earnings Dashboard

Daily/weekly/monthly earnings. Commission breakdown (gross, platform fee, net). Weekly settlements (daily for high-volume). TDS tracking (Section 194-O). Bank account management.

KYC & Verification

Aadhaar eKYC (DigiLocker/UIDAI API). GSTIN for businesses. Equipment RC + insurance + photos. Pesticide dealer license verification. "Verified Vendor" badge. Penny-drop bank verification.

Ratings & Reviews

5-star with sub-categories (quality, punctuality, equipment, behavior). Photo reviews from farmers. Response mechanism. Minimum 3 ratings before display. Fraud detection for fake reviews.

Individual Worker Profile

Skills listing (weeding, transplanting, harvesting, pruning, grafting). Daily availability toggle. Location + service radius. Preferred crops/work types. Daily wage rate (INR 300-600 by region). Experience & certifications.

Team Leader Dashboard

Manage team of 5-20 workers. Set team availability (blocks all members). Negotiate on behalf of team. Attendance management. Payment distribution (transparent breakdown). Handle substitutions for absent workers.

Job Discovery

Browse farmer job postings nearby. Filter by work type, duration, pay rate. Bid on jobs with custom rates. Accept direct bookings from farmers. Notification when new matching jobs posted. WhatsApp/SMS job alerts.

Work Tracking & Payment

QR code check-in at farm (GPS verified). Track hours/days worked. Completion confirmation. Direct payment to individual accounts (bypasses middlemen). Payment history. Tax summary for regular workers.

Section 08 — Edge Cases & Corner Cases

Every Corner Case Covered

Agriculture is messy. Weather kills plans, equipment breaks, workers don't show up, festivals happen. Here's how the platform handles every scenario.

Weather-Dependent Rescheduling

Spraying, ploughing, and harvesting can't happen in rain. Drone spraying needs wind <15 km/h. System integrates IMD weather API to auto-flag bookings 24-48h before when adverse weather is forecast.

Solution: "Weather Hold" status — neither cancelled nor confirmed. Auto-reschedule to next clear day. Full refund or free reschedule for weather disruptions. Proactive SMS: "Rain expected tomorrow, your booking auto-moved to April 18."

Seasonal Demand Spikes (5-10x)

During sowing (Jun-Jul Kharif, Oct-Nov Rabi) and harvesting, machinery demand spikes 5-10x. Supply can't meet demand.

Solution: Transparent surge pricing (max 1.5x cap). Advance booking discounts (book 2 weeks early = priority + 10% off). Waitlist with auto-assignment. Cross-region supply matching (move equipment from finished regions). FPO group bookings to optimize utilization across a village.

Group Labor — Workers Don't Show Up

Farmer books 10 laborers for 3 days. Only 7 arrive. Team leader takes cut, underpays workers.

Solution: QR check-in at farm location (GPS verified) for attendance. Platform pays individual worker accounts with transparent breakdown visible to farmer. Substitute policy: team leader arranges replacement within 2 hours or fee adjusted proportionally (-30% for 3 missing workers).

Equipment Breakdown During Rental

Rented tractor breaks down mid-job. Farmer loses critical time during harvest window. Who bears the cost?

Solution: Vendor must provide replacement within 4 hours or booking cancelled with full refund. Mandatory equipment fitness certification before listing. Breakdown reporting triggers emergency backup vendor assignment. Platform offers group insurance (INR 50/booking covers delays + damage). Downtime compensation to farmer if SLA missed.

Low Connectivity / Offline Booking

Many rural areas have intermittent 2G/3G. App may not load, transactions fail.

Solution: <15MB APK. Offline browsing with cached providers. SMS/USSD booking fallback (dial *123#). Queue actions and sync on reconnect. WhatsApp bot for booking (works on low bandwidth). PWA option for no-install users.

Platform Bypass (Direct Dealing)

After first transaction, farmer and vendor exchange numbers and transact directly, cutting platform out.

Solution: Mask phone numbers during initial bookings. Provide value-add that incentivizes staying: escrow protection, insurance, dispute resolution, payment tracking, digital receipts. Loyalty rewards for repeat on-platform transactions.

Advance Payment Fraud

Farmer pays advance, vendor never shows. Or vendor does work, farmer refuses to release payment.

Solution: Escrow model — money held by platform, not sent to vendor. Vendor penalty + automatic rebooking if no-show. Auto-confirm after 24-48h if farmer doesn't raise dispute. Tiered dispute resolution: auto → support mediation → senior escalation (48h ack SLA, 7-day resolution SLA).

Perishable Transport Delays

2-hour delay in vegetable/flower transport causes significant loss. Cold chain failure destroys produce.

Solution: Real-time GPS tracking of transport vehicles. Auto-escalation if vehicle deviates or delays beyond threshold. Temperature monitoring for cold chain vehicles. Transit insurance covering spoilage. Priority routing during harvest seasons.

Festival/Holiday Scheduling

Rural India has many local festivals where labor is unavailable. Varies by region, caste, religion.

Solution: Regional holiday calendar integrated into booking system. Auto-block known festival days for labor. Alert farmers booking near festival dates. Crowd-sourced local holiday data (vendors/workers report upcoming festivals).

Land Boundary Disputes

Vendor arrives for ploughing. Neighbor claims the land boundary extends into their plot.

Solution: Require land records (7/12 extract or equivalent) upload during booking for land-based services. GPS fence drawing for farm boundaries. Photo documentation before starting work.

Regulatory Compliance

Different states have different minimum wages for agricultural labor. Pesticide sales need licensing. Livestock transport needs health certificates.

Solution: Dynamic minimum pricing based on farmer's state. Vendor license verification during KYC (Insecticides Act 1968, Seeds Act 1966). Livestock transport health certificate upload. Interstate movement permits for livestock. GST compliance built into invoicing.

Insurance Coverage

Equipment damages crops. Rented cow falls sick. Produce damaged in transit. Who pays?

Solution: Mandatory equipment insurance for vendors. "Insure this booking" add-on (INR 50 covers equipment damage + delays). Livestock transit insurance. Government scheme enrollment (Livestock Insurance under DEDS, PMFBY for crops). Platform mediates insurance claims with partner insurers (ICICI Lombard, Bajaj Allianz).

Multi-Language for Illiterate Users

Many farmers are not literate even in their mother tongue. App text is useless.

Solution: Icon-heavy, pictorial UI for all service categories. Voice-first navigation (Bhashini API TTS/ASR). IVR fallback for feature phone users. Video tutorials for common flows. WhatsApp voice message booking support. Large touch targets (minimum 48px) for rough hands.

Section 13 — Village Resource Exchange

Village-Level Resource Sharing & Exchange

A hyper-local exchange platform where farmers within a village, panchayat, or taluka can share, barter, rent, and trade tools, fertilizers, water, seeds, livestock, and labor. Think "village WhatsApp group" but structured, searchable, and with payment protection.

💧

Water Resource Sharing

Farmers with borewells, ponds, or surplus water can list availability. Neighbors can request water for irrigation. Solves the critical water scarcity problem cooperatively.

Exchange Types:

  • • Borewell sharing — schedule hours per day, per acre pricing
  • • Pond/tank water — volume-based (per tanker load)
  • • Canal water turns — exchange/trade allocated canal slots
  • • Rainwater harvesting — share stored rainwater in dry months
  • • Drip system lending — rent drip kits between crop seasons
  • • Solar pump pooling — multiple farmers share one solar pump on a schedule
🌿

Fertilizer & Input Exchange

Surplus inputs from one season become waste. A farmer who bought excess DAP can sell to a neighbor at a fair price. Reduces waste, saves costs.

Exchange Types:

  • • Surplus fertilizer sale — leftover Urea/DAP/MOP at discounted rate
  • • Seed exchange — swap indigenous varieties between farmers
  • • Organic compost sharing — vermicompost, cow dung from livestock farmers
  • • Pesticide splitting — split large pesticide containers between small farmers
  • • Barter deals — trade compost for seeds, labor for fertilizer
  • • Group buying — pool orders for bulk discounts on inputs
🤝

Tool & Equipment Lending

Most small farmers own some tools but not all. A neighbor's sprayer sits idle 300 days/year. Enable village-level equipment sharing to maximize utilization.

Exchange Types:

  • • Hand tool lending — spades, sickles, hoes, axes (free or nominal)
  • • Power tool rental — sprayers, chaff cutters, generators (per day)
  • • Irrigation pipes — share pipes between crop seasons
  • • Storage containers — drums, bags, crates for harvest
  • • Protective gear — spray suits, masks, gloves
  • • Livestock exchange — breed cows, share bulls, loan poultry birds

Critical gaps (blockers to production)

No payment gateway implemented Blocker
Razorpay listed in architecture plans and Phase 3 roadmap but has 0% implementation. Without this, the entire marketplace monetisation is non-functional. Escrow, UPI, and payout flows are all missing.
→ Implement Razorpay before any vendor goes live. Route API for escrow is non-trivial — needs dedicated 2-week sprint.
Mobile app not wired to backend Blocker
React Native 0.81 app exists but is not connected to the 17 backend routes. TanStack Query is listed in the stack but no API calls are implemented. Vision Camera is listed but no ML inference pipeline exists.
→ Wire API calls before adding any new mobile features. Prioritise auth → prices → booking → notifications.
No real-time update mechanism Critical
Booking status changes (CONFIRMED → IN_PROGRESS → COMPLETED) require the farmer and vendor to see updates live. No WebSocket or SSE layer exists. Polling would destroy performance at scale.
→ Add Socket.io or Ably for booking room events. SSE is simpler for one-way notifications.
No async job queue Critical
SMS, WhatsApp, and push notifications are fire-and-forget in the current design. During peak harvest bookings (100s/minute), synchronous notification calls will block API responses and cause timeouts. Price ingestion from AGMARKNET also needs a queue.
→ Add BullMQ + Redis. Notification workers dequeue asynchronously. Prevents API latency spikes.
No caching layer Critical
Mandi prices are fetched from AGMARKNET and weather from IMD/OpenWeather on every request. These rarely change within minutes. Under load, this will hit external API rate limits and add 200–800ms latency to every dashboard load.
→ Redis cache for prices (TTL 15 min), weather (TTL 30 min), scheme data (TTL 24h). Cache-aside pattern.
PostgreSQL on Neon — connection limit problem Critical
Neon’s serverless PostgreSQL has connection limits (20–100 depending on plan). Express.js with Prisma holds persistent connections. At 50 concurrent users, you will hit connection exhaustion.
→ Add PgBouncer or use Neon’s built-in connection pooling mode. Set Prisma connection_limit=5 per instance.
No geo-search infrastructure Critical
The vendor discovery feature (“services near me within 5/10/25 km”) is the core UX. No PostGIS extension, no spatial index, and no geospatial query. This is the #1 query in the whole app.
→ Enable PostGIS on Neon. Add GEOGRAPHY columns. Create GiST index. Use ST_DWithin() for radius queries.
No offline-first implementation on mobile Critical
WatermelonDB is listed in architecture but NOT in the current mobile stack. The app currently requires network for every interaction. Rural users have intermittent 2G.
→ Add WatermelonDB now. Cache prices, vendor listings, and booking data. Sync queue on reconnect.
No observability / monitoring Critical
No error tracking, no APM, no structured logging. When the service goes down at 6AM during harvest season, there’s no alert, no trace, no log to debug.
→ Add Sentry (errors), Pino (structured logs), and uptime monitoring (BetterStack). Free tiers sufficient to start.

Document inconsistencies

OpenWeatherMap vs IMD — conflicting sources
Section 04 lists OpenWeatherMap as active; Sections 08-09 list IMD as target. IMD requires formal licence. No migration path documented.
→ Keep OpenWeatherMap for MVP. Plan IMD licensing in Phase 5.
Decimal fields missing precision in Prisma models
All financial fields use Decimal without precision/scale. Prisma defaults (65,30) cause slow aggregations.
→ Use @db.Decimal(15,4) for currency, @db.Decimal(10,2) for user-facing prices.
Mapbox GL & Sentinel Hub timeline conflict
Both listed as “plan” in current stack. Sentinel Hub costs ~$25/month minimum. It’s a Phase 7 feature listed as current.
→ Move both to Phase 7. Use free Mapbox free tier initially.
TimescaleDB migration strategy missing
Price history in main PostgreSQL. Architecture plans TimescaleDB as separate store. No migration path.
→ Start with TimescaleDB extension on same instance. Separate after 6 months.
Revenue projection math gap
10K bookings × INR 1,000 × 5% = INR 5L, not INR 50L. Math doesn’t add up.
→ Revise Year 1 target to INR 25L (commissions + vendor subscriptions).
KYC flow — DigiLocker/UIDAI unrealistic timeline
AUA/KUA licence from UIDAI takes 3–6 months. KYC scheduled Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4) — impossible.
→ Phase 1: manual KYC. Phase 3: KARZA/IDfy. Phase 6+: direct UIDAI.
Bhashini API “free” misconception
Requires ULCA registration. ASR quality poor for agricultural terms. Needs commercial ASR backup.
→ Bhashini for translation/TTS. Sarvam AI ASR for voice input until Bhashini matures.
TDS handling (Section 194-O) missing
Platforms must deduct 1% TDS on vendor payouts above INR 5L/year. Not in data model or roadmap.
→ Add TaxProfile model. Integrate Razorpay tax tools. Add to Phase 3 scope.

Missing systems (not mentioned, but required)

OTA Updates for Mobile
Bug fixes can’t wait 3-day Play Store review during harvest. Need Expo EAS Updates or CodePush for instant OTA deploys.
Image CDN + Compression
S3 raw upload without CDN = 3–8MB images over 2G. Need Cloudflare R2 + Images with WebP conversion.
Fraud Detection
Fake reviews, GPS spoofing for attendance, duplicate vendors. Need IP velocity checks, device fingerprinting, GPS validation.
Refresh Token Rotation
JWT-only auth. Stolen JWT valid until expiry. Need short-lived access tokens (15 min) + refresh tokens in DB.
Admin Panel (only 30% done)
KYC review, dispute resolution, vendor ban all need admin interface. At 30% it’s unusable. Blocks Phase 5.
CI/CD Pipeline
No deployment automation. Manual deploys at 3AM when booking service crashes. Need GitHub Actions + rollback.
