23. Plant Disease Detection System

The platform will provide AI-powered crop disease detection to help farmers identify plant diseases early and take corrective action.

Farmers can detect diseases through:

Mobile App Plant Scanner

WhatsApp Image Upload

Both methods will use AI image analysis combined with location-based weather data to generate an accurate report.

23.1 Feature Objectives

The objective of this feature is to:

Help farmers identify crop diseases early

Provide recommended treatments

Reduce crop losses

Use weather data and humidity conditions to improve disease detection accuracy

23.2 Disease Detection Methods
1. Mobile App Plant Scanner

Users can detect plant diseases directly inside the mobile application.

Flow

Step 1
User opens Plant Scanner feature in the app.

Step 2
Camera opens and the farmer scans the plant.

Step 3
AI analyzes the image.

Step 4
The system identifies:

Crop type

Disease type

Infection severity

Step 5
The farmer receives a detailed report.

Report Includes

Disease Name

Probability Score
Example:
Early Blight – 92% confidence

Affected Area

Severity Level

Mild

Moderate

Severe

Treatment Recommendations

Example:

Organic treatment

Chemical treatment

Preventive measures

Nearby Agricultural Stores

Recommended fertilizers / pesticides

23.3 WhatsApp Disease Detection

Farmers can also detect diseases using WhatsApp, which is useful for farmers who may not use the app regularly.

Flow

Step 1
Farmer sends a plant image to the platform's WhatsApp number.

Step 2
WhatsApp chatbot responds with instructions.

Example:

"Please enable location sharing and send your plant photo."

Step 3
User shares:

Plant image

Location

Step 4
AI system processes the image.

Step 5
User receives a detailed disease report via WhatsApp.

23.4 Location Requirement

To improve accuracy, location must be enabled.

Location is used to gather environmental conditions such as:

Temperature

Humidity

Rainfall

Soil moisture

Seasonal crop diseases common in that area

This improves AI prediction accuracy.

23.5 Weather Data Integration

The system collects weather data from APIs based on the user's location.

Weather parameters used:

Temperature

Humidity

Rainfall

Wind speed

Moisture levels

These parameters help determine:

Disease likelihood

Spread probability

Suitable treatment

23.6 Disease Intelligence Engine

The AI system will combine:

Image recognition

Weather data

Crop database

Regional disease patterns

To generate the final diagnosis.
